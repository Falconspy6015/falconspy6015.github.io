January 10, 2025 - A Day Full of Learning, Action, and Inspiration

Today was a perfect blend of progress in technical skills and unforgettable experiences at the Saarang cultural fest. Here’s how my day unfolded:

LeetCode & CTF Progress

I started the day with some problem-solving on LeetCode, tackling a couple of interesting questions, including a binary tree search problem that used recursive iteration. It was a fantastic challenge that made me appreciate the elegance of recursion in binary tree algorithms.

In addition, I spent a lot of time on Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges and hash cracking. I learned a lot about cipher cryptography, studying some fascinating ciphers, including Baconian cipher, Alien cipher, Columnar Transposition, and Pigpen cipher. These ciphers were a great reminder of the fun and complexity involved in cryptography!

Saarang Day 1 - Choreo Night

After working on technical stuff, I got ready and wore my Saarang merchandise to attend the first day of Saarang Fest. The event was absolutely amazing!

It was Choreography Night, and more than 20 teams from colleges across India, including IIT Madras and NIT Trichy, showcased their performances. Each dance was better than the last, and the energy in the air was just electric. The performances were so powerful, they gave me goosebumps!

I enjoyed a quick burger and pizza from the food stalls at the Open Air Theatre (OAT) before the rain made the show even more epic. Halfway through the final performance, it started raining, and the lighting lit up the entire stage through the rain while the dancers kept going, totally undeterred. It was one of the coolest things I’ve ever seen— everyone went wild, and the energy in the crowd was through the roof. The stage was literally on fire with the energy of the performance, and it felt unforgettable.

Productive Night - ROS & Notes

After the day’s festivities, I headed back to the hostel to dive into my work again. I spent some time simulating Turtlebot3 for my robotics project and organizing my Obsidian notes. It felt good to structure my thoughts and keep track of my progress.

I also wrote a self-help note on how to create and execute ROS projects more efficiently. The note provided clear steps, including the types of projects to focus on, and tips on how to handle challenges and improve productivity. To make coding smoother, I created code blocks in Markdown so that I can easily copy and execute them for various tasks.

Towards the end of the evening, I encountered a few issues with Gazebo, the simulation software I am using, but I’m currently troubleshooting and working to resolve them. It’s all part of the process, and I’m looking forward to seeing the improvements as I move forward.

Reflections

Today was a wonderful blend of productivity and excitement. On the technical side, I made great strides in CTF challenges, ciphers, and ROS work. On the cultural side, Saarang’s Choreography Night was an exhilarating experience that lifted my spirits and left me in awe of the performers’ talent. The combination of both aspects made this day memorable!
