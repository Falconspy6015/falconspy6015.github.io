January 14, 2025 - A Productive Day & Starting Semester 2 Preparations

Today was a blend of studies, planning, and tidying up for the start of Semester 2.

Morning: Studies in Linear Algebra

I spent a lot of time studying some important concepts in linear algebra:

  • Rank-Nullity Theorem
  • Solvability of Linear Systems
  • Linear Transformations and Basis
  • Change of Basis Matrix

These are all building blocks that will definitely come in handy as I progress in my courses, especially with the upcoming Semester 2.

Afternoon: Moving Back to the Semester 1 Room

After the fest stay, I shifted back to my Semester 1 room, vacating the temporary one I had occupied for Saarang. It was good to get back to my usual space.

Course Registration

Course registration was next on the agenda. I added and dropped a few courses. Unfortunately, I wasn’t able to secure any of the HS electives I had chosen initially. I had applied for three, but I didn’t get any of them. Instead, I added another one, hoping to at least get that. It’s a little frustrating, but it’s part of the system.

Night: Experimenting with OpenPyXL Library

Later in the evening, I dived into the OpenPyXL library to experiment with manipulating Excel files. I’m trying to personalize my Semester 2 timetable by organizing everything in a way that’s most efficient for me.

Other Notes: Happy Pongal!

Today also happened to be Pongal, a time for celebration. Wishing everyone a joyful Pongal! 🪔🌾

Looking Ahead: Semester 2 Begins

Tomorrow, everyone will start to head back to campus as Semester 2 officially kicks off on January 16. Things are about to get busy again, but I’m excited and ready to take on new challenges!
