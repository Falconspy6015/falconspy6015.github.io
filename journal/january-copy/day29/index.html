January 29, 2025 - A Day of Workshops, Cubing, and Groupwork 🛠️♟️

Class Ratings 📊

  • Physics – 4/10, another uninspiring session.
  • Signals & Systems – 4/10, same old struggle to stay engaged.
  • Digital Systems – 7/10, was the better of all as usual.

Workshop Time 🛠️

  • Changed into workshop clothes after lunch and headed for some hands-on action.
  • We were split into groups of three and tasked with replicating moulds using tools like cope, drag, runner, riddle, and trowel for metal casting.
  • The task was a blast! We divided and conquered, parallelizing our efforts to work efficiently as a team.

Cubing Practice: New Moves & Timing 🔥🧩

  • Spent the evening fine-tuning my cubing skills.
  • Worked on improving my timing, learned new permutations, and explored advanced methods. Progress is slow but steady—getting better with each twist.

Overall: A day full of productive workshop fun, teamwork, and some personal growth in cubing—ending on a high note of learning new techniques. 🚀
