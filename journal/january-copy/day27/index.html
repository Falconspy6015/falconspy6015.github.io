First Digital Systems Lab ⚡🔬

  • Worked on breadboards & NAND gates using the 7600LS IC.
  • Plotted voltage input vs. output—a simple yet fascinating experiment.
  • Key realization:
    • Nature operates entirely in analog, yet digital logic allows us to compute faster and more efficiently.
    • Someone once had the vision to go against nature’s preference and create digital computation—this thought truly stuck with me.
    • Might adopt this “go against nature to innovate” philosophy in my own life someday.
