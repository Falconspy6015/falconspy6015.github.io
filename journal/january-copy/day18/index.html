January 18, 2025 - Robotics Challenges and Triumphs

Morning & Afternoon: Endless Debugging

Continued working with the Raspberry Pi 4 and TurtleBot:

  • Spent a tiring morning flashing, reflashing, corrupting, and recorrupting the memory card. It felt like an endless cycle, testing my patience at every step.
  • Ran into several frustrating issues with storage integrity, but kept pushing through.

Evening: Progress Made

All the effort paid off!

  • Successfully set up teleoperation (teleop) for the TurtleBot, allowing me to manually control its movement.
  • Achieved autodock functionality, enabling it to autonomously return to its charging dock.

The sense of accomplishment was immense, and all the hard work felt worth it in the end.

Reflection
