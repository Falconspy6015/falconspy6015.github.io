January 12, 2025 - Saarang Day 3: Mixed Vibes and Rocking Nights

Today was a whirlwind of emotions, experiences, and energy as I continued soaking in the vibes of Saarang Fest 2025. Here’s how it unfolded:

CTF Disappointment

The day started with high hopes as I participated in the IIT Kanpur Web3-based CTF, but it turned out to be a letdown. Honestly, it didn’t even feel like a proper CTF. The focus was more on programming questions, and they didn’t even provide the full test cases for the challenges, which made it frustrating to solve. After a couple of attempts, I lost interest and decided not to bother proceeding further.

Exploring the Stalls

In the afternoon, I wandered around the Saarang stalls once more. I didn’t buy anything this time, but I enjoyed exploring all the different setups. The Himalayan Food Court was as lively as ever, brimming with delicious aromas and hungry festival-goers. There were also tons of aesthetic merchandise and handcrafted items on display, but I was more of a window-shopper today, just soaking in the festive vibes.

Rock Night: Mysore Express

The highlight of my day (and probably the whole festival so far) was Rock Night featuring the band Mysore Express. They absolutely rocked the stage, performing hit songs in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, and more—you name it! Their multilingual repertoire was mind-blowing and had the crowd roaring with excitement.

I had a fan pass, which meant I was literally standing right in front of the band, just a meter away from the performers. Watching them that close, with all the dazzling stage effects, glittering party popper gushers, and booming beats, was one of the most electrifying experiences I’ve ever had. I don’t think I’ll ever forget how surreal it felt to be in the midst of that energy.

Reflections

The day had its highs and lows, but the energy of Rock Night completely made up for the disappointments of the morning. Saarang has a way of bringing out moments you’ll treasure forever, and tonight was one of those moments. Here’s to another day of epic experiences tomorrow! 🌟
